About Helping Hand

Started by people who were tired of unreliable repairmen too.

Helping Hand began in 2018 with a simple frustration: it was too hard to find someone reliable to fix a leaking tap or a dead fridge without overpaying or waiting all day. We built a network of trained, background-checked technicians and gave every homeowner the same things we wanted for ourselves — a fair quote, a real time window, and a job done properly the first time.

Today, our team covers AC, cleaning, fans, refrigerators, carpentry, and plumbing — handling everything from urgent breakdowns to planned home maintenance.

  • Local technicians — based in your area, not dispatched across the city.
  • Genuine spare parts — with a warranty on every part we install.
  • No call-out games — the quoted visit charge is adjusted into your final bill.
